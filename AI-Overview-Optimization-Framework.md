# Core Principles of AI Overview Optimization

## 1. Direct Answer Formatting

AI systems prefer concise and semantically complete answers.

Example:

Question:
> "What is Semantic SEO?"

Strong AI Overview answer:

> "Semantic SEO improves search visibility by helping AI systems understand contextual relationships, entities, conversational intent, and topical relevance."

This format:
- answers directly
- contains entities
- improves retrieval quality
- satisfies intent quickly

# 2. Chunk Engineering

AI systems retrieve semantically complete chunks instead of entire pages.

Good AI retrieval chunks:
