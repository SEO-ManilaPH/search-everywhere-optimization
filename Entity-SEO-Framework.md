# What Is Entity SEO?

Entity SEO is the process of optimizing content around identifiable concepts, brands, people, locations, organizations, technologies, and contextual relationships.

Examples of entities:
- ChatGPT
- Google Search Console
- Manila
- Semantic SEO
- AI Overviews
- Ahrefs
- structured data
- OpenAI

Modern AI systems connect relationships between entities to understand meaning and context.
