# Topical Authority Framework

Topical Authority is the process of building deep semantic coverage around a subject to improve search trust, contextual relevance, and AI retrieval visibility.

Modern search engines and AI systems prefer websites that demonstrate:
- expertise
- semantic depth
- contextual completeness
- entity reinforcement
- conversational relevance

This framework explains how topical authority improves visibility across Google Search, AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, and modern AI-driven retrieval systems.

---

# What Is Topical Authority?

Topical Authority means becoming a trusted source around an entire topic ecosystem rather than targeting isolated keywords.

Traditional SEO focused heavily on:
- single keywords
- exact-match optimization
- isolated pages

Modern AI-driven search evaluates:
- topical depth
- semantic relationships
- contextual understanding
- entity relevance
- retrieval quality
