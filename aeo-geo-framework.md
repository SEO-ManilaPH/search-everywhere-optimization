# What Is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the process of optimizing content for:
- direct answers
- AI summaries
- featured snippets
- conversational search
- voice search
- AI-generated responses

The goal is to make content easier for AI systems to:
- extract
- summarize
- retrieve
- cite
- display

GEO helps AI systems understand:

# AEO Optimization Principles

## 1. Direct Answer Formatting

AI systems prefer concise and complete answers.

Example:

Question:
> "How long does buying a house in the UK take?"

Strong AEO answer:

> "Most UK property purchases take between 8 and 16 weeks depending on mortgage approvals, surveys, and property chain delays."

This format:
- answers directly
- contains entities
- satisfies intent
- improves retrieval quality

---

# 2. Conversational Search Optimization
